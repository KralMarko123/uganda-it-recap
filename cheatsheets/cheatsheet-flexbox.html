Flexbox Cheatsheet

display: flex; (turns flexbox on)
flex-direction: row; (items go left to right)
flex-direction: column; (items go top to bottom)
justify-content: start; (items at the start of main direction)
justify-content: center; (items centered along main direction)
justify-content: space-between; (items spread out along main direction)
align-items: start; (items at the start of cross direction)
align-items: center; (items centered along cross direction)
align-items: end; (items at the end of cross direction)
gap: 10px; (space between items)
height: 200px; (needed for vertical alignment)
display: flex; (always needed for flexbox)
